Privacy & Legal

Your files are never stored by StudioChief. Ever.

What happens to your files

  • Files are read locally in your browser and sent directly to Anthropic's Claude AI for processing.
  • StudioChief does not store, log, or retain your files, budgets, scripts, or cost reports — not even temporarily.
  • Anthropic processes your data under their API privacy policy. They do not use API inputs to train their models and do not retain your data beyond the immediate processing window.
  • Once your session ends, your data is gone from StudioChief entirely.

Anthropic's privacy policy

StudioChief uses Anthropic's Claude API to power its AI features. For details on how Anthropic handles API data, see anthropic.com/privacy.

No data sold. No ads. Ever.

StudioChief does not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way. There are no advertisers. Your production information stays yours.

03 — Flag Threshold
The Hot Sheet colors lines based on how far over budget they are. Set the base threshold — anything above this percentage gets flagged yellow. Lines further over get progressively warmer colors.
🟡 1–10% over → Yellow
🟠 11–20% over → Orange
🔴 21–30% over → Red-orange
🔴 31%+ over → Bright red
🔵 10%+ under → Blue
